Knowledge Gap Between LLM Research vs. GenAI Adoption

The late 2022 launch of OpenAI's ChatGPT marked a significant turning point for large language model (LLM) technology. It spurred a surge in activity within the academic research community, technology sector, and business industries. To quantify these impacts, we analyzed three data sources: arXiv publications for LLM research, Hugging Face Forum for LLM engineering and training, and OpenAI Development Forum for general AI (GenAI) applications.

We found that the rate of academic paper publication is twice as fast as the rate of questions asked in the Hugging Face Forum and 50% faster than the rate of questions asked in the OpenAI Development Forum. In other words, academic knowledge for LLMs is being created significantly faster than it can be absorbed by industries. This indicates a substantial knowledge gap between the academic world and industries. To accelerate GenAI adoption, we aim to facilitate this translation process
